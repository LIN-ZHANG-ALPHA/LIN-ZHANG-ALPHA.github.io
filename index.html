International Digital Economy Academy (IDEA)

Fu Tian, Shen Zhen

Lin Zhang is a Research Scientist currently affiliated with the International Digital Economy Academy (IDEA). He specializes in the latest developments and trends in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Large Language Models (LLMs), and how they can be applied to solve complex real-world problems. He has published dozens of research papers in prestigious venues, including ICML, ACL, KDD, CVPR, and others. Additionally, he has collaborated with several industry leaders to develop cutting-edge solutions that have served billions of users on social networks. In his current role, he leads research projects, mentors junior researchers, and contributes to the development of new technologies.
